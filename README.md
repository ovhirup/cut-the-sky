# Cut the Sky

*Guide your kite. Tighten the line. Cut a path through the sky.*

A tiny kite-fighting score-attack game for macOS (Apple Silicon native),
celebrating the Indian Independence Day kite-flying tradition — paper
craft, colour, wind and the "Kai Po Che!" joy of a clean cut. Built in 27
days by one developer pair-programming with Claude, targeting release on
**August 15, 2026 — India's Independence Day**.

> Screenshot/GIF coming once the sky fills with kites (Week 2).

## How it plays

Move the pointer to steer your kite. Hold the button to tighten your
ribbon: a tight ribbon cuts other kites' lines — but hold too long and
your own line snaps. Runs last one or two minutes. The sky gets more
chaotic every ten seconds. One more try.

- **Engine:** Godot 4 · **Platform:** macOS (Apple Silicon, 60 FPS on M1)
- **Distribution:** itch.io, free with optional payment
- **Design spec:** [DESIGN.md](DESIGN.md) · **Dev log:** coming with the
  first teaser
